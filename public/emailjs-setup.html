1

Create EmailJS Account

  1. Go to https://www.emailjs.com/
  2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
  3. Enter your email address and create a password
  4. Verify your email address (check your inbox)
  5. You'll get 200 free emails per month on the free tier
2

Add Email Service

  1. After logging in, go to "Email Services" in the left sidebar
  2. Click "Add New Service"
  3. Choose your email provider:
    • Gmail - Most common, easy setup
    • Outlook - Microsoft email
    • Custom SMTP - For other providers
  4. Follow the connection instructions for your provider
  5. For Gmail: You'll need to enable "Less secure app access" or use an App Password
  6. Once connected, you'll see your Service ID (e.g., service_abc123)
  7. Copy this Service ID - you'll need it in Step 5
3

Create Email Template

  1. Go to "Email Templates" in the left sidebar
  2. Click "Create New Template"
  3. Give it a name: "Payment Reminder"
  4. Set the Subject to: {{subject}}
  5. Copy and paste this template content:

Template Content:

Dear {{to_name}}, {{message}} Payment Details: - Amount Due: Ksh {{amount}} - Due Date: {{due_date}} - Days Until Due: {{days_until}} Please ensure payment is made on time to avoid any inconvenience. Thank you, Mweshpra Apartments Management
  1. Click "Save"
  2. You'll see your Template ID (e.g., template_xyz789)
  3. Copy this Template ID - you'll need it in Step 5
4

Get Public Key

  1. Go to "Account" → "General" in the left sidebar
  2. Scroll down to find your Public Key
  3. It looks like: abcdefghijklmnop (a string of letters/numbers)
  4. Copy this Public Key - you'll need it in Step 5
5

Test the System

  1. After saving your configuration, click "Test Email" above
  2. Enter your email address in the test form
  3. Check your inbox for the test email
  4. If you receive it, the setup is complete!
  5. Payment reminders will now be sent automatically when tenants log in
