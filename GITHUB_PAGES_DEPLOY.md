# Deploy to GitHub Pages (Free)

## Steps:
1. Go to your repository: https://github.com/preston-254/mweshpre
2. Click "Settings" tab
3. Scroll down to "Pages" in the left sidebar
4. Under "Source", select:
   - **Branch:** `main`
   - **Folder:** `/public`
5. Click "Save"
6. Wait 2-3 minutes
7. Your site will be live at: `https://preston-254.github.io/mweshpre/`

## Note:
- You may need to update all your internal links to use relative paths (they should already be relative)
- The URL will be: `https://preston-254.github.io/mweshpre/`

## Benefits:
- ✅ Completely free
- ✅ Works on mobile and desktop
- ✅ Automatic deployments
- ✅ Custom domain support (in settings)
